Flight Planning

Create and deploy optimized flight plans to efficiently scout any region of your operation. Plan your flights at home or in the field using our iOS App (coming soon!) Whatever your choice, you'll enjoy the ease of use and end-to-end intelligence only Skycision can deliver.

Aerial Mapping

While your drone is flying, our app is mapping! Our aerial mapping platform captures elevation data to estimate yield and multispectral imagery to assess crop health and vigor. Aerial mapping saves time in crop scouting and helps to pinpoint areas of your field that are in need. Improve resource allocation and maximize yield with the touch of a button!

Rapid Insights

By aggregating data from various operations regionally and nationally, we can better understand the factors that contribute to crop performance in various climates and regions. The results of the large scale analysis allow us to understand and benchmark operations at a local level to deliver rapid insights to our farmers. Let us help you in maximizing the value of our mapping capabilities.

Technologies

We are working closely with industry leader DJI to develop software for Phantom III, Inspire II and Matrice 100 UAVs. Specialized imaging sensors measure crop health, generate elevation maps to estimate yield, and generate Prescription Files for precision agriculture. Accessible, low cost drones in combination with our mapping application make collecting aerial data easier than ever, allowing farmers to detect and react to threats in near real-time.
